Maldives

Maldives is a tropical state in the Indian Ocean, located on 26 ring–shaped atolls, which consist of more than a thousand coral islands. It is famous for its beaches, blue lagoons and huge reefs.

The Maldives has a stable tropical monsoon climate (Am) in accordance with the Köppen climate classification, which is influenced by a fairly large area of South Asia. Since the Maldives has the lowest altitude among all countries in the world, the temperature is constantly high and humid.
